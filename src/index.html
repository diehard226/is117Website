Welcome

Do you know what the internet really is? Do you know what is before we had DSL and everything else? Most people just remember the internet from when they had dial up and they had to either use the internet or get off the phone. The internet was just developing and becoming what we know and use today. But the concept of the internet was well alive before then. In facts, it started back in 1958.

Why we Exist.

This website is here to help you gain a quick understanding of the history of the internet and the people who shaped it. Our How it works pages explains the how opening your browser and typing into the search bar starts the connection to connect to servers for the web pages you visit. The History page contains a nice timeline and dates for the most important milestones in web history.
